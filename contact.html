    ITALIAN CRAFTSMANSHIP MEETS TECHNOLOGY

  • Established in 1971, ARTWOOD has created a niche for itself as the leading OEM/ODM producer for furniture components across the world panorama. We are a fully integrated product design and manufacturing partnership offering turnkey product solutions directly to our clients, without the hassle of dealing with middlemen. An OEM/ODM for all major vendors, we offer our clients with, accelerated product development cycles, rigorous cost control, an obsessive quality focus and a number of industry quality certifications.
  • Operating in a fully-atomized state-of art facility in Italy, our capabilities and proven processes encompass everything that is required to provide our clients with bespoke products that meet the highest standards of quality and craftsmanship.
